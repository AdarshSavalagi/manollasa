About course

Bachelor of Business Administration [B.B.A.] is a 3 year course providing skills, knowledge and training in professional management and entrepreneurial abilities. The undergraduate degree course can be studied from all backgrounds. BBA courses are available in various specializations such as Finance, Marketing and HR Management. BBA Syllabus is designed to impart adequate business administration skills to the students. BBA Subjects are Principles of Management, Macroeconomics, Human Behavior & Ethics at Workplace, Management Accounting, Banking & Insurance. BBA 1st year subjects include Financial Accounting, Macroeconomics, Microeconomics, Quantitative Techniques etc. BBA course is very market and industry oriented. Students after completing the course get good BBA Jobs. The average BBA salary ranges between INR 2,50,000 to 10,00,000. There are various types of BBA courses in India like BBS, BMS, BBA-FIA, BBA(Hons) etc. Courses after BBA includes students doing higher studies in MBA with various MBA specialisation such as MBA in Finance, MBA in HR, MBA in Marketing etc.

Eligibility

  • Candidates should have passed the 10+2 exams from a recognised education board by the university
  • The general category aspirants should have scored a minimum of 50% marks as an eligibility condition for the BBA entrance exam while the SC/ST candidates should have a score of 45% at least.

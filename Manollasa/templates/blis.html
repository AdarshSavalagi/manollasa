About course

BLIS stands for Bachelors of Library Science and Information is a UG level degree program that is completed in a duration of 1 year. Through this course, students become enough capable of managing and administrating the library. There are two study semesters within the complete duration. Mainly the preservation and maintenance of libraries are concerned with the learning of this course.Most of the subjects included in the BLIS syllabus are Library Classification, Library Cataloguing, Patriarchy and General Inequalities, Computer Applications in LICs, Library, and Society, Information sources and Services, etc. There are multiple top public and private colleges where one can study BLIS degree courses. Most probably the admission process for BLIS starts every year in May.

Eligibility

  • The candidate must complete his graduation in any discipline from a recognized university.
  • The candidates must have a minimum of 50-55% marks in his/her graduation/
